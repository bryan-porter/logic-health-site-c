Cross‑training gives small teams breathing room during vacations, illness, and surges. Here's a **4‑week curriculum** to get front desk staff productive in billing support—without losing them to jargon overload.

## Week 1: Foundations
- Revenue cycle overview in **one page**
- Patient types, payers, deductibles, copays
- **Eligibility checks** and common failure codes
- Scripts for missing referrals, expired coverage

## Week 2: Scheduling & Auth
- CPT vs diagnosis basics
- Which visits/procedures **require prior auth**
- Opening PA cases and uploading attachments
- Reschedule scripting for missing PA

## Week 3: Charges & Edits
- Charge basics, place of service, **modifiers**
- Front‑end edits you can fix (demographics, coverage, auth numbers)
- When to escalate to coder

## Week 4: Communication & Resilience
- Handling difficult calls
- Protecting PHI in everyday workflows
- Daily **huddle** and **handoff notes** templates

## Small hospital tips
- Create a shared **knowledge base** across clinics.
- Shadow RCM for one day; return with three fixes to scheduling scripts.

## Success signals
- Fewer same‑day cancellations
- Higher **clean claim rate**
- Lower front‑end edit volume
