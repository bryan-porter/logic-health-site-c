# Pricing

## Principles
- **Transparent:** No hidden fees
- **Aligned:** Pricing matches outcomes and scale
- **Sustainable:** Built for long‑term programs, not short pilots

## Common Models
1. **Per‑Enrolled‑Patient (PEPM):** Flat monthly rate per active CCM/RPM patient
2. **Performance‑Aligned:** Base PEPM + success component tied to adherence or outcomes
3. **Hybrid:** Lower PEPM with tiered volume discounts

## What's Included
- RN/MA care team for outreach/triage
- Documentation, time tracking, and monthly summaries
- Device provisioning and logistics (RPM)
- Program reporting and quarterly optimization

## Not Typically Included
- Replacement devices due to loss/damage beyond normal wear
- Exceptional custom development or one‑off integrations
