# Compliance & Security

## Our Commitments
- **HIPAA program & BAA:** Covered‑entity workflows with signed BAAs
- **Access control:** Least‑privilege roles, audited activity
- **Data protection:** Encryption in transit and at rest; secure device provisioning
- **Vendor management:** Risk assessment and oversight for critical suppliers

## Documentation Integrity
- **Minutes tracking:** CCM vs RPM separated, never double‑counted
- **One‑provider rule:** Safeguards to prevent multiple CCM/RPM billers in the same month
- **Audit trail:** Source notes, timestamps, and monthly summaries

## Quality Management
- Protocols with clinical leadership oversight
- Periodic chart audits and remediation
- Denial monitoring and root‑cause analysis

## Policies & Training
- Workforce privacy/security training
- Incident response and breach notification procedures
- Annual policy review cadence
